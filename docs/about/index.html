Purpose

All of the assumptions of statistical analysis rarely hold. So the challenge for the pragmatist is to understand when evidence is strong enough to support action. That’s where sensitivity analysis comes inâ€”so we can understand how robust our inferences are to challenges to our assumptions. One example is a statement such as:
